Built for the industry,
by the industry

HospitalityAgents exists because no one else was building this. A vertical marketplace for the AI workflows hotel operators actually need.

Why this exists

The general-purpose AI agent marketplaces are full of tools built by engineers for engineers. A skill for setting up tmux loops doesn't help a revenue manager identify rate compression. A generic CEO persona doesn't know the difference between ADR and RevPAR.

Hospitality is a $1.5 trillion industry with deeply specific, high-stakes workflows — and most AI tools treat it as an afterthought. HospitalityAgents is the marketplace where that changes.

Our principles

🏨

Vertical-first

Everything here is built specifically for hospitality. No generic tools dressed up with hotel emoji.

⚙️

Platform agnostic

Listings work with any major AI provider. We don't lock operators into a single vendor.

✓

Operator-tested

Every listing goes through a review. We prioritize configs used in real hotel environments.

💰

Creator-first economics

Creators keep 90% of every sale. We grow when they grow — and not before.

Who builds here

Former operators who know the workflows, hospitality tech founders, AI engineers who've gone deep on the vertical, and consultants who've automated their own processes and want to package them.
